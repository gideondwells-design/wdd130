Indiana

Indiana is a beautiful state with serval claims to fame. so times known as "the Hoosier State," Indiana located in the Midwestern region of the United States and is the 19th state of the USA. It is bordered by Lake Michigan and Michigan to the north, Ohio to the east, Kentucky to the south, and Illinois to the west, making it a key transportation hub often referred to as the "Crossroads of America." The capital and largest city is Indianapolis, which is famous for hosting the Indianapolis 500, a major automobile race. Indiana's economy is diverse, with significant contributions from manufacturing, agriculture, and services.
